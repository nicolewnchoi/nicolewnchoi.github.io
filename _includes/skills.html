Skills

Hi there! My name is Wanpil Nicole Choi. I am an undergraduate pursuing a dual degree in computer science and art & design at the University of Michigan-Ann Arbor. I explore and build new ways to combine both disciplines of engineering and art to create pleasurable experiences for the user and promote humanity in the age of technology.

This is reflected in my projects where they center around the wellbeing of the user. For example, using mixed reality to create a detailed visualization of the pain points to help the users communicate their illness better and augmented reality to promote users’ wellbeing through nurturing virtual plants.

I’m currently focused on XR development, game development, digital animations, and learning artificial intelligence and UX/UI of web pages.

When I’m not working, I love solo traveling, exploring new restaurants, drawing digital art, skiing, and playing video games!
